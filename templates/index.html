Biofloc Fish Farming - Biofloc Technology Microbial Formulation

Biofloc technology is a technology that removes waste created by aquatic animals and provides nutrition to aquatic animals

  • Which species are best for Biofloc fish farming?

    Two main factors to consider before selecting species for Biofloc are: Species that are able to consume floc directly and gain nutrition. Species that can tolerate high solids concentration in water.

Biofloc technology is a system to convert waste and toxic materials (like Ammonia, Nitrate, Nitrite) to useful matters which can also be used as feed for fish. By recycling proteins, Biofloc system helps to enrich water quality and reduce the risk of disease outbreaks. Probiotics used in Biofloc Technology play a significant role by destroying harmful bacteria in water. An important advantage of Biofloc system is that it requires zero or limited water exchange under high stocking density.
